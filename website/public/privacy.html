Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2023

Companion ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Companion.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture (if using Google Sign-In).
  • Health Data: To generate personalized diet plans and track progress, we collect your height, weight, date of birth, gender, and dietary preferences.
  • User Content: Information you post to the Community Threads or logs you create for meals.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To create your account, generate AI-based diet plans, and track your nutrition.
  • AI Analysis: We use Google Gemini AI to analyze food logs and generate recommendations. Anonymized data may be processed to provide these results.
  • Communication: To send you important account updates (e.g., password resets, email verification).

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our app:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database storage, and hosting.
  • Google Gemini API: For generating nutritional insights and diet plans.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures designed to protect your information, including encryption of data in transit and at rest via Google Firebase. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

5. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data (including health metrics, logs, and posts) directly within the app via the Security & Privacy settings ("Danger Zone").

6. Children's Privacy

Companion is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Companion Team.
